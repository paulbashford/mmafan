Points System

Our points system rewards the users knowledge of mma and how much risk theyre willing to take. Users have got to take a fighter's potential fight value into account versus their opponent. Our algorithm takes the fighters previous 5 fights, the fighters takedowns per fight on average and the fighters strike accuracy into account to generate their potential points for a win. From the last five of each fighter's fights, 1 point is added to their potential score for each loss and 1 point is taken away for each win. The algorithm also adds and deducts points where relevant to the fighter that has a worse overall strike and takedown accuracy
